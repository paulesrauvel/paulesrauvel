Graphic Design

Graphic design is the art and practice of planning and projecting ideas and experiences with visual and textual content. It involves the creation of visual compositions to communicate messages effectively. Designers use typography, imagery, color, and layout to craft visual content that meets specific needs, such as branding, advertising, and digital media.

Video Editing

Video editing is the process of manipulating and rearranging video shots to create a new work. It involves selecting the best footage, trimming segments, adding transitions, effects, and audio, and creating a cohesive final product. Video editing is a crucial part of post-production in filmmaking, television production, and digital media.
